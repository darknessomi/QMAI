# Webnovel Character Fit

## Core Principle

Character design must serve the plot engine and selling point. Start from what the story requires the protagonist to do repeatedly, then infer personality, weakness, strategy, and growth.

## Execution

1. **Read the plot and hook**
   - Ask: what behavior will this story reward?

2. **Infer the protagonist engine**
   - If the hook rewards indirect control, the protagonist may be cautious, clever, shameless, or strategic.
   - If the hook rewards confrontation, they may be decisive, hot-blooded, or stubborn.

3. **Add contradiction**
   - Give the protagonist a useful trait and a flaw created by the same trait.

4. **Define repeated behavior**
   - Describe what the protagonist does again and again that makes the book recognizable.
